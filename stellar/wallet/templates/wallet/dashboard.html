Where is the money stored?

On the Stellar network, funds exist on the network and can only be moved by whoever has the secret key. This means that your secret key is extremely sensitive, and whoever has access to it can move the funds. However, money is NOT actually "inside" Interstellar. Interstellar is a tool that helps you use your secret key to make transactions.

WARNING: Be extremely careful with your secret key and do not share it with anybody.

The Stellar network requires accounts to maintain a minimum balance. A 1 XLM minimum balance is required with an additional requirement 0.5 XLM for each entry in the account such as a trustline or offer. Each entry (asset accepted, offer, signer) increases your minimum balance by 0.5 XLM. Additionally, Interstellar enforces a 0.5 XLM of extra minimum balance in an attempt to make sure your account can still make transactions without going below the network minimum balance requirements. To decrease your minimum balance, you can remove an existing offer or unaccept an asset.
